  3. HAM AND CREAM CHEESE ROLL UPS

Recipe #1

  • Time: 40
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Cups flour 2
Teasspoon salt 1/2
Teasspoon Baking powder 2
Cup oil 1/2
Cup sugar 1/3
Eggs 2
Cup milk 1
Teasspoon vanilla essence(optional) 1

DIRECTIONS

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375f or 190c; line muffin cups with papers.

Step 2:Cream butter and sugar till light and fluffy (make sure the butter is room temp so the mixture doesn't clump) . Beat in eggs one at a time.

Step 3: Add flour (mixed with baking powder and salt) alternating with milk beat well; stir in vanilla.

Step 4: Divide evenly among pans and bake for 18 minutes. Let cool in pans and enjoy! :).
